Aims and Objectives

  • Uplifting Worship: To uplift, promote, and maintain the standard of music and worship in the church and beyond.
  • Fellowship and Unity: To encourage and foster the spirit of fellowship, progress, unity, and collaboration among fellow choristers and the Pretoria Anglican Fellowship (PACF).
  • Organizing Recreation: To organize activities that offer recreation to the church community.
  • Participation in Festivals: To take part in music festivals and competitions organized by the parish, PACF, or other church-related organizations.
  • Support in Grief: To assist and attend prayer meetings and funerals for fellow parishioners.
  • Visiting the Sick: To visit sick congregants at home and in hospitals, providing support and comfort.
  • Fundraising: To raise funds to support the choir and the parish.
  • Stewardship and Bible Study: To display good stewardship, encourage Bible study among members, and lead by example within the diocese.
