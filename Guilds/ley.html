Frequently Asked Questions

Lay Ministers assist in church services by reading scriptures, distributing Holy Communion, and offering pastoral support within the congregation.

You can express your interest by speaking with the parish priest or guild leader. Training and approval by the church are typically required.

While formal theological training is not mandatory, Lay Ministers usually undergo basic instruction and spiritual formation provided by the church.
