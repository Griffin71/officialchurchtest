St. Agnes Guild Aims and Objectives

  • Uphold Truth: To help members believe in and adhere to what is true.
  • Promote Goodness: To encourage members to do what is right and to turn away from all that is harmful.
  • Active Participation: To nurture loyalty among members, inspiring them to be active participants in the Church of God.
